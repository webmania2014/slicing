Privacy Policy

Scope of this Policy

Brightergy, Inc. (”Brightergy”) understands you care about how information about you is collected and used. Brightergyis committed to protecting the privacy of individuals who interact with Brightergy. This Privacy Policy describes Brightergyprivacy practices for our websites, services, and apps that link to this policy.

In this Privacy Policy, we will refer to the Brightergyhosted services and related apps collectively as our "Services." We will refer to our emails, newsletters, texts, and other marketing and advertising practices collectively as our "Communications."

Information collected by BRIGHTERGY

Brightergy registration and customer support

You may choose to provide Brightergy information when you register to use a Brightergy website or Service, request a Services demonstration, contact Brightergy for support or other offerings, or attend an event or forum in which Brightergy is participating. During such processes, Brightergy collects personal information that identifies you. Brightergy may collect, for example, your name, company name, address, email, phone number, financial, billing or payment information, position, or title.

Brightergy website and use of cookies

Brightergy receives and stores certain information about how you use our websites and Services when you visit a Brightergy website or use our Services. For example, we receive information that your browser or device sends to our servers whenever you visit a Brightergy website. Your browser or device may tell us your internet protocol (IP) address used to connect your computer to the Internet, computer and connection information such as browser type, version, language and time zone setting, browser plug-in types, operating system, and type of device you are using. When you visit a Brightergy website, your browser may also tell us information such as the actions you take on our website, the page that led you to our website and, if applicable, the search terms you typed into a search engine that led you to our website.

Brightergy receives information about how you use our websites and Services through the use of first-party and third-party cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies. Our servers collect similar information when you are logged into our website or Services. The information Brightergy receives through cookies and similar technologies may be anonymous or it may be associated with you, depending on the website or Services you are using.
